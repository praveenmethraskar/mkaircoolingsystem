About MK Air Cooling System

established in 2007

MK Air Cooling System is a trusted manufacturer and service provider specializing in high-quality HVAC solutions in Hyderabad, Telangana. With over a decade of industry experience, we deliver reliable, efficient, and cost-effective air cooling and ventilation systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.


We offer a comprehensive range of products and services including exhaust blowers, exhaust hoods, air conditioning duct systems, HVAC ducting, and fabrication services. Our expertise also extends to ductable air conditioners, central air conditioning systems, and custom duct fabrication, ensuring tailored solutions that meet diverse client requirements.


Under the leadership of Mohd Abdul Razzak, our company has built a strong reputation for quality workmanship, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction. With a skilled team of 11–25 professionals, we are committed to maintaining high standards in every project we undertake.
